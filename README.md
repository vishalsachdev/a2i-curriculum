## Vision

The A¬≤I program embraces a foundational insight: ***capability is cheap; coherence is scarce***. 

In a world where AI tools proliferate rapidly, the scarce skill is not mastering individual technologies but orchestrating them into coherent systems that deliver sustainable value while maintaining governance, trust, and inclusion. This program prepares leaders who can navigate this complexity through reflexive learning, ethical practice, and strategic systems thinking.

## Program Structure

### 36 Credits | 12 Courses | 8-Week Format

Designed for working professionals, the program delivers twelve 8-week courses combining asynchronous learning, applied projects, oral vivas, and AI-augmented feedback.

#### Core Courses (24 credits)
Eight foundational courses establish shared literacy in orchestrating intelligent systems, with business-focused additions:

- **A¬≤I 601 ‚Äì AI Strategy & Problem Framing**: Position AI initiatives, vendor evaluation, ROI analysis, build-vs-buy decisions
- **A¬≤I 602 ‚Äì Data & AI Infrastructure**: Design data architectures, data monetization, multi-vendor management
- **A¬≤I 603 ‚Äì Evidence & Causality**: Experimental methods, business impact assessment, ROI measurement
- **A¬≤I 604 ‚Äì Predictive & Prescriptive AI**: Balance automation with judgment, platform economics, pricing strategies
- **A¬≤I 605 ‚Äì Intelligent Agents & Workflows**: Architect multi-agent systems, change management, human-AI teams
- **A¬≤I 606 ‚Äì Governance & Trust Systems**: Regulatory requirements, COBIT/ISO frameworks, compliance
- **A¬≤I 607 ‚Äì AI Economics & Business Models** *(New)*: Platform economics, pricing strategies, market dynamics, competitive positioning
- **A¬≤I 608 ‚Äì Leading AI Transformation** *(New)*: Change management, building data cultures, executive communication, innovation labs

#### Applied Studios (9 credits)
Three project-intensive studios provide hands-on practice:

- **A¬≤I 621 ‚Äì Analytics Engineering Studio**: Build inclusive data pipelines and semantic layers on Google Cloud
- **A¬≤I 622 ‚Äì Data Product Studio**: Prototype deployable AI products with stakeholder review
- **A¬≤I 623 ‚Äì Agent Ops & Evaluation Studio**: Evaluate agentic workflows for reliability, safety, and observability

#### Electives & Domain Tracks (3 credits)
Mission-driven tracks with industry partnerships contextualize core capabilities:

- **Growth for Good**: Sustainable growth metrics, ESG integration, responsible marketing (Partners: P&G, Patagonia, Shopify)
- **Finance for Inclusion**: Regulatory compliance, alternative credit, risk management (Partners: Community banks, fintechs, Federal Reserve)
- **Healthcare for Humanity**: HIPAA compliance, clinical decision support, health equity (Partners: Hospital systems, Epic, FDA)

Plus **A¬≤I 643 ‚Äì Innovation Leadership** offering three pathways: Corporate Innovation (60%), Venture Creation (25%), Social Innovation (15%).

#### Capstone (3 credits)
**A¬≤I 699 ‚Äì Intelligent Systems for Public Value**: Synthesize program threads through either a systems integration project with an organization or a venture launch supported by the iVenture Accelerator.

## Pedagogical Approach

A¬≤I pedagogy unites six complementary models:

- **Reflexive Learning**: Students co-create knowledge artifacts, critique platforms, and reflect on the systems they build
- **Cyber-Social Learning**: Human ‚Üî AI ‚Üî peer feedback loops sustain continuous improvement
- **AI Feedback and Assessment**: Intelligent tools provide formative coaching rather than compliance policing
- **Empowerment ‚Üî Plasticity Framework**: Transform classrooms into adaptive ecosystems
- **Capabilities vs Skills**: Architect reusable capabilities rather than master transient tools
- **Business Coherence Scorecard**: Measurable framework using established business tools (Balanced Scorecard, Six Sigma, COBIT/ISO, Stage-Gate) to assess and improve coherence

This design widens the program's ***coherence bandwidth***‚Äîthe shared capacity of humans and machines to learn from and with one another‚Äîwhile providing concrete business metrics for measuring orchestration capability.
