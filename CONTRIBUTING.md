# Contributing to A2I Curriculum

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the AI for Innovation curriculum!

## How to Contribute

### Reporting Issues

Use the issue templates to report:
- Curriculum feedback
- Bug reports
- Feature requests
- Article reviews

### Suggesting Changes

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Submit a pull request

### Curriculum Updates

For curriculum changes, please:
1. Use the curriculum update issue template
2. Provide clear rationale for changes
3. Include learning outcomes impact
4. Reference relevant pedagogy

### Submitting Articles for Review

Found an interesting article, blog post, or research paper relevant to AI education? We welcome article submissions!

**How to Submit:**
1. Use the [Article Review issue template](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/article_review.yml)
2. Provide the article URL and basic information
3. Explain why it's relevant to the curriculum
4. Suggest how it might be integrated

**What Happens Next:**
- Your submission is automatically labeled and routed to the curriculum team
- Initial triage within 2-3 business days
- Full review within 1-2 weeks (depending on urgency)
- If integrated, a detailed review document is created in `docs/research/`

**Types of Articles We Review:**
- Research papers and preprints
- Industry blog posts and case studies
- Technical tutorials and guides
- News articles about AI trends
- Opinion pieces on AI ethics and policy
- White papers from industry leaders

See [docs/research/README.md](docs/research/README.md) for more about our article review process.

## Review Process

All contributions are reviewed by:
- AI-assisted initial review
- Faculty curriculum committee
- Student feedback integration

## Style Guide

- Use clear, accessible language
- Include practical examples
- Reference academic sources where appropriate
- Follow markdown formatting standards

## Questions?

Open an issue or reach out to the curriculum team.
