# Innovation Studio & Venture Ecosystem

## Mission

The Innovation Studio translates A²I’s entrepreneurship thread into a year-round incubator that helps learners turn reflexive insights into ventures aligned with public value. It leverages the Origin Ventures Office, iVenture Accelerator, and Strategy & Innovation faculty network highlighted in the founding conversation.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L124-L135】

## Participation Pathways

- **Venture Track Learners:** Students pursuing the A²I 699 Venture Launch capstone secure residency to advance their startups with mentorship and resources.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L116-L135】
- **Domain Track Collaborators:** Teams from Growth for Good, Finance for Inclusion, and Healthcare for Humanity electives incubate sector-specific pilots that extend mission impact.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L111-L114】【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L156-L163】
- **Community Partners:** Civic and industry partners co-locate for limited residencies to co-design solutions with student teams, reinforcing engagement and economic development commitments.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L156-L163】

## Venture Lifecycle

1. **Sense (Weeks 0-1):** Teams frame mission-aligned opportunities, grounding problem statements in UIUC’s access purpose and stakeholder interviews.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L27-L47】【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L135-L163】
2. **Design (Weeks 2-4):** Prototypes emerge via GCP sandboxes, with GitHub issues tracking design decisions and AI partners suggesting coherence checks.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L65-L109】【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L139-L152】
3. **Evidence (Weeks 5-6):** Teams run experiments, measure causal impact, and stress-test governance assumptions with mentors and peers.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L94-L135】
4. **Reflect (Weeks 7-8):** Ventures host viva-style sessions with investors, mentors, and community partners, capturing learning for public reports.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L76-L120】【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L135-L177】

## Equity & Inclusion Lens

Residency selection prioritizes ventures advancing access, inclusion, and regional economic development, reflecting program threads and land-grant obligations. Stipends and childcare support reduce barriers for underrepresented founders.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L39-L47】【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L156-L163】

## Governance

- **Stewardship Board:** Faculty, alumni founders, and partners review studio progress quarterly, issuing public memos on ecosystem health.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L214-L230】
- **Open Metrics:** Venture milestones, funding raised, and community impact dashboards publish to GitHub Pages for transparency.【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L167-L193】【F:docs/context/conversation.md†L214-L230】
