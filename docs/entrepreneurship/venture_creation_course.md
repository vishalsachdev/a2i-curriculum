# Venture Creation Course

## Overview

The Venture Creation course is a hands-on, experiential course where students develop AI-powered business ideas from concept to prototype, learning entrepreneurship through practice.

## Course Objectives

Students will:
- Identify problems that AI can solve
- Develop viable business models
- Build functional prototypes
- Validate ideas with real users
- Pitch to stakeholders
- Understand startup ecosystem

## Course Structure

### Module 1: Opportunity Identification
**Duration**: 2 weeks

#### Topics
- Problem discovery
- Market research
- Customer interviews
- AI capabilities assessment
- Opportunity evaluation

#### Activities
- Customer discovery interviews (10+ conversations)
- Problem statement development
- Competitive analysis
- Opportunity pitch (2 minutes)

#### Deliverables
- Problem statement
- Market research report
- Opportunity assessment

### Module 2: Solution Design
**Duration**: 3 weeks

#### Topics
- Design thinking
- AI solution architecture
- User experience design
- Technical feasibility
- MVP definition

#### Activities
- Ideation workshops
- User journey mapping
- Technical spike
- Prototype sketching
- Feedback sessions

#### Deliverables
- Solution design document
- Technical architecture
- MVP specification
- User stories

### Module 3: Business Model
**Duration**: 2 weeks

#### Topics
- Business model canvas
- Revenue models
- Customer acquisition
- Unit economics
- Competitive positioning

#### Activities
- Business model development
- Financial modeling
- Pricing strategy
- Go-to-market plan

#### Deliverables
- Business model canvas
- Financial projections
- Pricing strategy
- Market entry plan

### Module 4: Prototype Development
**Duration**: 4 weeks

#### Topics
- Agile development
- AI model development
- Product development
- Testing and iteration
- Technical documentation

#### Activities
- Sprint planning
- Development sprints
- User testing
- Iteration based on feedback
- Demo preparation

#### Deliverables
- Working prototype
- Technical documentation
- User test results
- Product roadmap

### Module 5: Validation and Pitch
**Duration**: 2 weeks

#### Topics
- Customer validation
- Metrics and KPIs
- Investor pitch
- Storytelling
- Presentation skills

#### Activities
- Beta testing
- Metrics collection
- Pitch deck development
- Pitch practice
- Final presentations

#### Deliverables
- Validation report
- Pitch deck
- Demo video
- Final presentation
