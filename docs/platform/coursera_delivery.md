# Coursera Delivery

## Overview

Selected A2I curriculum courses are made available on Coursera to reach a global audience and provide flexible, open access to AI education.

## Course Offerings

### Public Courses

Courses available on Coursera:
- Introduction to AI and Machine Learning
- AI Ethics and Society
- Practical Machine Learning
- AI for Innovation

### Specializations

Multi-course sequences:
- AI Fundamentals Specialization
- Applied AI Specialization
- AI for Business Leaders

## Course Format

### Video Content
- Professional production quality
- Modular, 10-15 minute segments
- Closed captions and transcripts
- Multiple languages (planned)

### Assessments
- Auto-graded quizzes
- Programming assignments with auto-graders
- Peer-reviewed projects
- Final assessment

### Hands-On Labs
- Browser-based coding environments
- Cloud-hosted Jupyter notebooks
- Real-world datasets
- No local setup required
