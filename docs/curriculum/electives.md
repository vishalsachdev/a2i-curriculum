# Electives

## Overview

Electives allow students to customize their learning path based on interests and career goals.

## Available Electives

### Technical Electives

#### Advanced Machine Learning
- Ensemble methods
- Transfer learning
- Meta-learning
- AutoML

#### AI Systems Engineering
- ML ops and deployment
- Model serving at scale
- Monitoring and maintenance
- Production ML systems

#### Robotics and Embodied AI
- Robot perception
- Motion planning
- Human-robot interaction
- Autonomous systems

#### Generative AI
- GANs and VAEs
- Diffusion models
- Large language models
- Creative AI applications

#### Quantum Computing for AI
- Quantum fundamentals
- Quantum algorithms
- Quantum ML
- Near-term applications

### Domain-Specific Electives

#### AI for Healthcare
- Medical imaging
- Clinical decision support
- Drug discovery
- Healthcare analytics

#### AI for Finance
- Algorithmic trading
- Risk assessment
- Fraud detection
- Financial forecasting

#### AI for Climate and Sustainability
- Environmental monitoring
- Climate modeling
- Resource optimization
- Sustainable systems

#### AI for Education
- Adaptive learning systems
- Educational data mining
- Intelligent tutoring
- Learning analytics

### Cross-Disciplinary Electives

#### AI and Law
- AI regulation
- Intellectual property
- Liability and responsibility
- Legal applications of AI

#### AI and Design
- Human-centered AI
- UX for AI systems
- Design thinking
- Creative applications

#### AI and Business
- AI strategy
- Business transformation
- AI product management
- Value creation

## Elective Format

- Typically 4-6 week intensive courses
- Mix of lectures, readings, and projects
- Guest speakers from industry and academia
- Practical, application-focused

## Adding New Electives

The curriculum is designed to evolve. New electives can be proposed through the curriculum update process. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../../CONTRIBUTING.md) for details.
