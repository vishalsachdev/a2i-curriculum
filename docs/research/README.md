# Research Articles & Reviews

## Purpose

This directory contains reviews and analyses of research articles relevant to the A2I curriculum. These reviews help:

- Identify emerging trends in AI/ML research
- Incorporate cutting-edge research into curriculum design
- Provide students with curated, relevant academic resources
- Track the evolution of AI technologies and methodologies

## Structure

Each article review should include:

1. **Article Metadata**
   - Title
   - Authors
   - Publication venue (arXiv, conference, journal)
   - Date
   - Link to article

2. **Summary**
   - Brief overview of the research
   - Key findings and contributions

3. **Relevance to Curriculum**
   - Which courses/modules could benefit from this research
   - Specific topics or concepts covered
   - Potential learning outcomes

4. **Analysis**
   - Strengths and limitations
   - Practical implications
   - Pedagogical considerations

5. **Recommendations**
   - Suggested integration points in curriculum
   - Additional resources or readings
   - Potential lab exercises or assignments

## Contributing

When adding a new article review:

1. Create a markdown file named: `YYYY-MM-arxiv-ID.md` (for arXiv papers) or `YYYY-MM-author-title.md` (for other papers)
2. Use the template provided in `article-review-template.md`
3. Link the review in the index below
