It reflects broader trends in how established financial institutions are adapting to the AI revolution and the changing skill requirements in the finance industry.

## Research Overview

### Problem Statement

The article addresses the evolving relationship between traditional financial institutions and AI companies, specifically examining how major banks like Goldman Sachs are positioning themselves in the AI era through talent acquisition and strategic partnerships.

### Methodology

- Traditional finance is increasingly dependent on AI capabilities, particularly LLMs
- The skill set required in finance is rapidly shifting toward AI literacy and development
- Strategic partnerships between finance and AI companies are intensifying
- New career paths emerging at the intersection of finance and AI technology
